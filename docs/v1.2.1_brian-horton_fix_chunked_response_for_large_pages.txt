Date: 2026-03-19

Issues:
  1. Admin console HTML page (48KB) caused RTL8139 NIC driver panic:
     "Unable to get TX buffer" at src/drivers/net/rtl8139.rs:550.
     Root cause: write_response() sent the entire 48KB+ response in one
     write_all() call, creating a massive TLS record. This generated more
     TCP segments than the RTL8139's 4 TX descriptors could buffer,
     causing the kernel to panic.

Changes:
  1. Modified http::write_response() to handle large responses:
     - Small responses (under 4KB): sent in one shot as before (single
       TLS record, reliable for smoltcp)
     - Large responses (over 4KB): headers sent first, then body written
       in 4KB chunks with flush after each chunk. This keeps each TLS
       record small enough for the RTL8139 to handle.
     - Threshold of 4KB chosen to stay well within the RTL8139's TX
       buffer capacity while minimizing round trips.
